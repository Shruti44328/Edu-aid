The 1st decade of online education has just started. From a home tuitor to every big universities, each one of them are in need to switch their teaching platforms from offline to online. EDU-aid is a very powerful customised teaching application which can be used in multiple educational industries to manage their entire online class room facilities. It comes with live, saved video tutorial platform along with versatile test series, notes, student resgistration and e-book selling portals.
